National Decarbonisation Mandates (Brownfield Retrofit)

Concrete phase-out deadlines and renewable-share thresholds de-risk retrofit capex.

All seven governments have now fixed – or are actively consulting on – hard stop-dates for high-carbon district-heat, making brownfield retrofit a "must-do", not a "nice-to-have".
Country Coal / fossil phase-out for DHNs Mandatory low-carbon threshold for legacy grids Additional brown-field provisions
Germany Coal-CHP shut-down 2038 (law) – ambition 2030; 17-20 GW_th to replace. From 2024, building code (GEG) forces ≥65% RES content for any new heat supply; existing DHNs must reach ≥50% RES or waste-heat by 2030 under BEW funding rules. Municipal heat-plans mandatory (cities >100k inh. by 2026) define retrofit zones.
Poland Utilities (PGE, Tauron, Veolia) publicly commit to coal-free DH by 2030; anti-smog bylaws ban coal boilers in major cities the same year. Fit-for-55 "efficient DH" rule: ≥50% RES or ≥80% CHP by 2026. ETS cost: 41.5 bn zł CO₂ fees in 2023 – direct retrofit trigger.
Czech Rep. Government coal exit accelerated to 2033 (was 2038). Same EU 50% RES / 80% CHP threshold; Modernisation Fund "HEAT" subsidies reserved for assets that meet pathway to 2030 targets. Steam-to-hot-water mandate – 130 km already rebuilt since 2014; requirement embedded in grant contracts.
France Ban on new oil & coal boilers (July 2022); coal in DH <1% already. Networks must stay >50% EnR&R to keep 5.5% VAT & "classé" status (automatic since 2022). "Classement" gives municipalities legal power to force connections during major building renovations.
United Kingdom No coal in DH; Gas boilers banned in new homes from 2025; Clean-heat policy de-facto obliges low-carbon retrofit of gas-CHP grids. Ofgem framework (2025-26) to licence operators – licence condition will require credible 2050 net-zero plan; HNES grants tied to ≥50% CO₂-reduction. Zoning pilot cities can mandate existing buildings to connect or convert.
Hungary Coal virtually eliminated (<1%); Green DH Program targets ≥50% RES share by 2030. Profit-cap uplift to 9% applies only to renewable retrofit projects, creating a financial stick. Regulated tariffs + 5% VAT keep customer base stable during transition.
Spain No explicit coal legacy; but draft Royal Decree will require ≥50% RES for existing DHNs to gain "efficient" label & GoO. Fossil-boiler ban (EPBD transposition) phases in from 2027 for large buildings. PERTE retrofit grants prioritise (>30% scoring) fuel-switch of legacy gas grids to biomass or heat pumps.
