Analytical perimeter – precise object under study

"Only local-loop ECs (RES ≤ 1 MW, storage ≤ 3 MWh, one LV/MV PCC) deliver traceable value – wider 'virtual suppliers' are out of scope."

Decision axis Accepted in perimeter Explicitly excluded Primary legal / factual reference
Physical layout • Single public-grid point of common coupling (≤ 36 kV)
• All producers/loads inside that "island" balance internally before import/export		 • No physical proximity (national balancing groups; e.g., Enercop FR virtual offer) ES RD 244/2019, art. 4 §3 (LV/MV autocons.), IT DLgs 199/2021 art. 31 (same MV/HV substation)
Asset mix • Rooftop/ground PV ≤ 1 MW (per RED II recommendation)
• Li-ion storage ≤ 3 MWh (median 200 kWh – see Slide 2)		 • Pure FiT plants selling 100 % to wholesale market Project fact sheets in Slide 2 (20 pilots 2020-24)
